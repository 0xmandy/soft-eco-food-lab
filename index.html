01

Who We Are我们是谁

In a world of food surplus, where no one goes hungry, we explore the relationship between people and food — why a human still eats at all. For FoodLab, what lets a dish linger beyond the memory of taste is recollection and imagination. 在一个食物过剩、没有饥饿的世界里，我们探索人和食物的关系——人为什么还要吃饭。对 FoodLab 来说，能让一道菜停留得比味觉记忆更久的，是回忆与想象。

So FoodLab doesn't produce food — we awaken, record, and connect (feed) the flavors that live only in memory. Every flavor we bring back is filed into the Flavor Library of the 67th World. This time, we've brought the lab to the 67th World Opportunity Fair — not to sell a product, but to find the people who still understand why this bite tastes good. 所以 FoodLab 不生产食物——我们唤醒、记录、连接（feed）那些只活在记忆里的味道。每一段被找回的味道，都归入第 67 世界的风味图书馆。这一次，我们把实验室搬到了第 67 届世界机遇展——不是来卖产品的，是来寻找那些依然懂得"为什么这一口好吃"的人。

02

Three Open Roles三个开放岗位

Role · 01
Role I / 岗位一

Flavour Finder风味唤醒师

"Find the flavour out — the one buried in your memory."把味道找出来——那一种，藏在你记忆里的。

Task / 你要做的

Bring back a flavour that lives only in your memory. Name it, describe why it tastes good, and record it so the lab can file it into the Flavour Library.找回一种只活在你记忆里的味道——说出它、描述它"为什么好吃"，并记录下来，让实验室把它归入风味图书馆。

Role · 02
Role II / 岗位二

Flavour Scout招募官

"Find the right person out."把对的人找出来。

Task / 你要做的

Spot the people who still understand why a bite tastes good, and bring them in. Pitch a role to someone, win them over — recruit the right person for the Flavour Library.找出那些依然懂得"为什么这一口好吃"的人，把他们带进来——向某个人讲述一个岗位、说服他，为风味图书馆招到对的人。

★ Reward: recruit a player, earn one extra free drink. / 成功"招到"一名玩家，可多得一杯免费饮料。
Role · 03
Role III / 岗位三

Flavour Feeder喂养者

"Feed the flavour out — to someone else."把味道喂出去——给别人。

Task / 你要做的

Feed a flavour to another person. Through the act of "feeding" (feed), connect two people who carry different tastes — and seat them together at one mutual table.把一种味道喂给另一个人。通过"喂养（feed）"这个动作，把味觉记忆不同的两个人连接起来，让他们坐到同一张"共餐桌（mutual table）"前。

In the 67th World, food no longer matters. What matters is whether the person holding this cup of tea still remembers what eating means. 在第 67 世界，食物已经不重要了；重要的是，端起这杯茶的人，还记不记得吃的意义。

Every flavour we bring back is filed into the Flavour Library of the 67th World.每一段被找回的味道，都归入第 67 世界的风味图书馆。
